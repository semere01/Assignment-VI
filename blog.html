    How are you doing with the COVID-19 quarantine?

    Well, to be honest, I always spent my time at home even before all of this happened. Apart from going to campus for class lectures and lab sessions, I’d never find any reason to leave the comfort of my own home. To answer the question, with the quarantine in play, I have found myself trying to learn basics of backend web development. I am also progressing the working knowledge I have in Android Development. All of this keeps me occupied and lets the days go by without any anxiety over the state of the nation.

    What are your plans for the next 5 years?

    There are so many different tangents one could take in this field; I’m not sure about which path I want to take. My top picks however would be something along the lines of systems engineering or backend development. These areas intrigue me because the opportunity of flexibility and optimization they provide. Right now however, I am focusing on getting through college with good grades. After that, I hope to see myself working in one of these areas.
